About Us

Welcome to G.H.S Kot Ram Dass

Located in Jalandhar, G.H.S. Kot Ram Dass has been providing quality education for over 35 years. As a proud institution under the Department of Education, we are committed to nurturing young minds and preparing them for a bright future, all while instilling values of discipline, integrity, and community spirit.



Academics

Our school provides a comprehensive and enriching academic program for students from Grade 6th to Grade 10th. With a commitment to academic excellence, we offer a balanced curriculum designed to challenge and inspire students to become critical thinkers and lifelong learners.

Curriculum Overview

Mathematics
Our mathematics curriculum builds strong problem-solving skills and logical thinking. Students progress from basic arithmetic in early grades to advanced topics such as Algebra, Geometry, and Calculus

Science
Our science curriculum fosters inquiry and exploration. Students study fundamental concepts in Earth, Life, and Physical Sciences in earlier grades, advancing to subjects like Biology, Chemistry, and Physics

English Language
The English program develops reading, writing, and communication skills. Students engage with diverse literature, improve writing techniques, and participate in discussions to enhance critical thinking and creativity.

Social Studies
The social studies program teaches history, geography, and civics. Students gain a global perspective through the study of past events, cultures, and modern societal issues, preparing them for informed citizenship.

Languages: Hindi/Punjabi
Students learn Hindi and Punjabi, building proficiency in speaking, reading, and writing. Cultural understanding is integrated through literature

Physical Education
Physical Education promotes fitness and teamwork through sports and physical activities. Students participate in a variety of sports and learn about healthy living, nutrition, and wellness.
